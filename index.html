Cybermarine delivers specialized engineering solutions across offshore assets including FPSOs, FSOs, FLNGs, and Floating Production Units. Our expertise spans the full project lifecycle—from concept and FEED to detailed engineering, integration, and conversion.

With a strong foundation in naval architecture, hull structural engineering, hydrodynamics, and marine systems design, we have successfully supported complex offshore projects for global clients such as BW Offshore, TotalEnergies, Keppel Shipyard, and Husky CNOOC.

Our Capabilities Include:
  • Hull and marine system design and detailed engineering
  • Global and local structural analysis, fatigue and strength assessment
  • Stability, weight estimation, and regulatory compliance (ABS, DNV, NORSOK)
  • 3D modelling, integration engineering, and production detailing
  • Marine P&IDs, system calculations, and technical documentation

Cybermarine has also demonstrated strong problem-solving capability through innovative engineering solutions—optimizing safety systems, improving layouts, and reducing operational constraints without compromising compliance.

Our track record across new builds, conversions, and FEED studies positions us as a reliable engineering partner for offshore developments worldwide.
